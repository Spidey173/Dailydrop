Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about DailyDrop deliveries

How do I place an order?

Browse our catalog, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Enter your delivery address and confirm payment. Your order will be delivered in 10–15 minutes!

What are the delivery charges?

Delivery is completely free for all orders, no matter the cart size. We believe in transparent pricing with zero hidden charges.

How long will my order take?

Orders are typically delivered within 10–15 minutes during operating hours. Peak times may affect delivery times slightly, but we always aim to be as fast as possible.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept Cash on Delivery (COD). Pay conveniently when your order arrives at your doorstep — no online payment needed.

What is your return and refund policy?

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, contact us within 2 hours of delivery. We'll issue a full refund or send a replacement at no additional cost.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us via email at support@dailydrop.com or call us at +91 78292 83748. Our support team is available daily from 7am to 11pm IST.
